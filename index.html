Sadaqah: Donate to Humanity First

The Palestine Appeal

The conflict between Israel and Gaza has led to immense suffering, with thousands of lives lost, many injured, and millions displaced. Civilians in Gaza face dire conditions, including damaged schools, health facilities, and shortages of essential supplies. Humanity First (HF) is actively providing emergency aid, including medication, food, and water, and has been instrumental in offering free medical care to thousands.

Masroor Center in Yopougon-Gesco

Humanity First has been diligently working on a new hospital in Yopougon-Gesco, near Abidjan, Ivory Coast. This facility will include an Emergency Department, Maternity suites, Neonatal care, an ICU, and Operating Theatres, along with comprehensive diagnostic and support services. With the main structure completed, the team is now focused on the internal fit-out, including infection control flooring and basic infrastructure. Donations are urgently needed to complete the medical gas infrastructure and install medical equipment. Supporting this project will ensure the hospital can provide essential healthcare services to the community.
